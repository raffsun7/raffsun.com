How much is the presence of Islamic values ​​in our lives? How much do we think about our death and afterlife in everyday life? But our daily activities are affected around these. Fear of God is created in the heart. The book 'Written in Tears' is organized with the stories of some real events that will remind us of death and remember the afterlife. Bela Furabar Age

How many springs of life have passed, how many dawns have faded. Like a dim memory, life slowly fades into darkness. Simple numbers of time are decreasing gradually. All birds return to the nest. All rivers return to estuaries. Yet some people, overcome by delusion, do not want to return. By breaking the bonds of delusion and maya, they cannot run on the path of eternal eternity.

Ma, Ma, Ma & Baba

Those who are dearest to us in this world, whose debts cannot be repaid in any way, are our mothers and fathers. Every child should serve them at all times. This book is a collection of wonderful stories about the consequences of disobedience to parents, the rewards of obeying them, and how we should treat them.
